Worthy Jerky

Sales & Account
Associates
Worthy Jerky distributes through multiple channels that require specific account managers to balance the work-load. These include but are not limited to: supermarkets, distributors, campus stores, gyms/fitness centers, hotels, and golf courses. More specifically, Worthy Jerky needs:
  • A) Greek Accounts- Several Greek organizations purchase Worthy Jerky through SMC. We need someone to maintain these accounts and serve as a point of contact between Worthy Jerky, Greek houses and SMC.
  • B) Campus Stores Account- Worthy Jerky products will be sold in all major retail locations on campus. We need someone to maintain these accounts and serve as a point of contact.
  • C) New Client Acquisition - Worthy Jerky will be expanding rapidly over the next few months. We need an outgoing, personable individual to identify and acquire new wholesale clients. They will be responsible for maintaining these individual accounts.
Market Research Associate
  • Market Research Associates conduct market analysis for all levels of competition and conduct early stage research for new wholesale client acquisition. They will assist the Business Development Associates with acquiring and maintaining wholesale accounts.
Digital Media Marketing Associate
  • Digital Media Marketing Associates will manage Worthy Jerky’s social media accounts (Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, Instagram) and marketing channels like MailChimp to engage customers and build brand loyalty.
Event Planning Associate
  • Worthy Jerky conducts sampling events in coordination with it’s wholesale partners. Duties for these projects would include planning logistics for the events to ensure Worthy Jerky makes a strong showing and is adequately equipped to meet the demand of the crowd present. These events have led to acquisition of new wholesale clients in the past.
Graphic Designer
  • Graphic Designers collaborate with the marketing associates to produce marketing materials for both online and physical channels such as packaging designs, advertisements, banners, information packets (for wholesale customers), product pictures, and more. They also work closely with the web-design sub-team to design and update the company website (www.worthyjerky.com) as needed.
Lab Research Assistants
  • Lab Research Assistants help the R&D Director(s) in exploratory research in extending the shelf life of Worthy Jerky products. An ideal candidate would have a strong background in microbiology and lab techniques as well as an interest in food science and food products.
